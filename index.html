Quick Answers for Curious Minds

How do you create an Ad on Poruwa.lk?

Creating an ad is simple! Register for a free account, fill in your profile details including personal information, education, profession, and partner preferences. Upload photos and submit for verification. Your ad will be live once approved.

Who can post an Ad?

Anyone seeking a life partner can post an ad. You can register yourself, or family members (parents, siblings) can create profiles on your behalf. Professional matchmakers can also create and manage multiple profiles.

What's the Ad cost?

Creating a basic ad is completely FREE! You can upgrade to premium plans starting from LKR 1,200/month to unlock features like viewing contact details, unlimited messaging, and profile boosting.

Can parents create accounts for their children?

Yes, absolutely! Parents, guardians, or family members can create and manage profiles on behalf of their children. Simply select "Father", "Mother", or "Sibling" when registering.

Can you remove your Ad?

Yes, you can deactivate or permanently delete your ad at any time from your account settings. You can also temporarily hide your profile if you need a break.

What features are included in Premium?

Premium includes unlimited profile views, viewing contact details, sending unlimited interests, chat with matches, advanced search filters, see who viewed your profile, and priority customer support.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our smart matching system analyzes your preferences including age, religion, profession, location, and lifestyle choices to suggest highly compatible matches with a percentage score.

Can I verify my profile?

Yes! Upload a valid government ID to get a verified badge on your profile. Verified profiles get more responses and appear higher in search results.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@vivahaBandhu.lk, call our hotline +94 11 XXX XXXX, or use the live chat feature on our website. Our team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

What if I find a fake profile?

Please report suspicious profiles using the "Report" button on their profile. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action to maintain platform integrity.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if you haven't used any premium features. Please contact support with your transaction details for processing.
